## Vocabulary

Use this list to see the acceptable spelling and punctuation for terminology in the UI.

The vocabulary is listed in alphabetical order.
However, terms starting with the words _Coveo_, _for_, or _on_ are alphabetized by the next distinguishing word (company, feature, or product name).
For example, _Coveo for Salesforce_ is listed under S for _Salesforce_.
