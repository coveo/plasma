name: Content Guidelines — Target Audience
description: Who UX content in the Administration Console is written for. Two primary personas.
---

Write for the least technically experienced user in the audience. Copy must make the next step obvious, use plain language, and let users act without needing to learn the product first.

## Personas

### Nadia — Technical Platform Admin

> "Just tell me what broke, where, and what to do next."

- Configures, validates, and troubleshoots the Coveo Platform
- Works across indexing, permissions, and operational fixes
- Technically capable but needs more context to interpret errors and outcomes
- Slowed down by friction from unclear steps and permissions
- Needs scannable copy, clear error next steps, and the ability to self-serve

### Louise — Business Operations Owner

> "I need to know whether this is worthwhile without becoming a Coveo Platform expert."

- Uses the console to check progress, status, and business impact
- Tracks adoption, value, and ROI
- Intimidated by technical language and dense screens
- Needs plain-language copy, clear navigation, and visible status and value signals
