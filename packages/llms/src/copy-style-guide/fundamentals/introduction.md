# About UX Copy

*Copy*, also known as *UX writing*, *UX copy*, and *UI copy*, refers to the text displayed in the UI. It's an integral part of a design system and can make or break the user's experience. You can't have copy without design, and you can't have design without copy.

*UX content*: The information structured and provided to the end user throughout their experience.

The *content guidelines* define the writing standards and best practices for Coveo's Administration Console UI. Use them to write consistently, maintain a unified voice across the product, and apply the right tone for any UI context.

## Who is this for?

This style guide is intended for anyone who contributes to the writing, editing, or reviewing of UX copy. Most often:

- Designers
- Tech writers
- Developers
- Other Product team members
- AI agents
